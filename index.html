Episodes

One on One, hosted by Jack Acello and Noah Schwartz, is an all-encompassing basketball podcast, specifically focused on the NBA. Jack and Noah, long-time college roommates, spend much of their free time watching, analyzing, and debating the NBA, and now they get to bring those conversations to the airwaves! Be sure to tune in weekly for some of the best basketball content available!

  • May 10, 2022

    NBA Conference Semifinals review and predictions, and review of HBO Max series, Winning Time: Rise Of The Lakers Dynasty - Season 1 Episode 10

    This week on One on One, Jack and Noah discuss some of the games in the NBA conference semifinal round in the playoffs and where they think each series will go. They also discuss season 1 of the hit show on HBO Max, Winning Time: Rise Of The Lakers Dynasty and how it told the story, some of the actors portray and where the show will go in season 2.

    Jazz needs a reboot, Grizzlies cruise right along, and the All-NBA Injured/Excuse teams - Season 1 Episode 5

    This week on One on One, Jack and Noah discuss three of the most disappointing teams in the NBA in the Jazz, Lakers, and Nets, as well as three up-and-coming teams that could play a part in the title chase in the Mavs, Grizzlies, and Celtics. Jack and Noah also close out the show with a fun segment, giving out their All-NBA teams for injured and excuse-making players.
