Happy 2024! Knicks-Raps Trade, Darvin Ham on the Hot Seat, Nets Are a Mess, Trade Deadline Predictions - Season 3 Episode 14

Jan 7, 2024

One on One is back for 2024 with a jam packed show. Joined by Lucas Jafet of Share The Rock, the guys talk the Knicks-Raptors trade and how OG Anunoby has given New York the type of player they desperately needed. Then, they break down the Lakers recent struggles and Darvin Ham’s uncertain future as head coach. Next, discussions on the issues with the Nets, the immense success of the Clippers, and the Pistons finally ending their historic losing streak. The guys close the pod with some trade deadline predictions, including what the Thunder and Warriors might do, and a debate on where Dejounte Murray will play the rest of the season.
